Humaira Science and Techincal Collage

Junior Secondary School (JSS)

The Junior Secondary School offers a broad-based curriculum designed to provide a strong foundation in core subjects such as Mathematics, English Language, Basic Science, Social Studies, and Civic Education. Students also engage in introductory courses in technical and vocational subjects.

Senior Secondary School (SSS)

Electrical Engineering (ELE)

Focuses on the principles of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism.

Mechanical Engineering (MEC)

Covers the fundamentals of mechanics, kinematics, and thermodynamics.

Automobile Engineering (AUT)

Provides training in vehicle design, manufacturing, and maintenance.

Building Technology (BLD)

Focuses on construction principles, materials, and techniques.

Woodwork Technology (WDW)

Offers instruction in carpentry and woodworking.

Plumbing and Pipefitting (PLB)

Prepares students for careers in plumbing and pipe installation.

Computer Science/ICT (CSC)

Provides knowledge of computer systems, programming, and IT.

Painting and Decorating (PNT)

Teaches the art and techniques of painting and decorating.

Welding and Fabrication (WLD)

Focuses on welding techniques and metal fabrication.

Radio and Television Electronic (TRV)

Covers principles related to radio and television systems.
